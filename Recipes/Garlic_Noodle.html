Garlic Noodle

A flexible and simple noodle dish, a perfect carb bomb.

Are you tired of eating pasta with tomato sauce and alfredo sauce ? Try this asian variation.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce.
  • 1 taablespoon oyster sauce.
  • 2 teaspoons Worcestershire sauce.
  • 2 teaspoons fish sauce.
  • 1/4 teaspon sesame oil.
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper.
  • 8 cloves garlic,minced.
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter.
  • 1/4 cup parmigiano-reggiano cheese.
  • 1 tablespoon chopped green onion.
  • 6 ounces spaghetti or any noodles in a equivalent amounts.
  • 1 pinch red pepper falkes.

Steps

  1. In a small mixing bowl, put soy sauce, oyster sauce, Worcestershire suace, fish sauce, sesame oil, and cayenne pepper.

  2. Sitr the ingredients.

  3. Melt buter in a skilet over a medium heat. Add garlic, cook till about 1 minute. Till garlic is fragrant.

  4. Add the sauce mixture to the skilet, and turn off the heat.

  5. Cook the sphagetti or the noodle of your choice.(follow instructions on the box)

  6. Transfer the sphagetti/noodle to the sauce.

  7. Mix the conent well, add in some pasta/noodle water as needed to make the dish more creamy.

  8. Add in the cheese and Garnish with red pepper falkes and green onions.

  9. Note: This dish is very flexible. Think of it as a blank canvas.Enjoy!
