Chickpea Toast

A toast loaded with avocado and roasted chickpea.

Do you like avocado? Do you like chickpeas? Do you like bread? Well here it is.

Ingredients
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil
  • pinch of kosher salt
  • 1 avocado
  • 2 slices of bread of your choice

Steps
  1. In a mixing bowl, toss in chickpeas with paprika,olive oil,and some salt.

  2. Mix to combine.

  3. Toast the chickpea mixture till the cickpeas are done cooking.

  4. Slice up some avocado, and spread evenly onto the bread of your choice.

  5. Top up the toast with roasted chickpeas.

  6. Check for seasoning and enjoy!
