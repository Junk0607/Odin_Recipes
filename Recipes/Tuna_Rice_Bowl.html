Tuna Rice Bowl

A quick and easy, delicious tuna rice bowl recipe.

A simple dish for the broke college student. A perfect balance between protein and carb for the broke.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Tuna
  • 1 tbs Kroger Lite Mayo
  • 1/3 cup Markukan Organic Seasoned Rice Vinegar
  • 2tbs lite soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup of green onions
  • 1 cup of cooked white rice
  • 2 teaspoons Siaracha hot sauce
  • 1/2 lemon juice for the taste
  • 1 pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 teaspoon of seasame oil.

Steps

  1. Pour rice into a pot, and add water. (Make sure to wash the rice first).Bring to a simmer over a medium heat reduce heat.Cover the pot and conntiue to simmer for 15-20mins.

  2. Place tuna into a large mixng bowl and break up with using fork. Toss in 1/4 cup of green onions, 1/3 cup of rice vinegar, 1tbs lite mayo, seasame oil, Siracha and lemon juice.

  3. Fluff rice with fork and trasnfer the rice into the mixing bowl.

  4. Mix thoroughly with a fork until ingredients are incoprated.

  5. Taste and adjust seasoning. Toss in red pepper falkes and green onion to your liking and enjoy!
